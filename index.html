About Me

Hello! I am a first year M.Eng - Cybersecurity student at UMD. In the past, I worked as a Security Engineer at we45 Solutions Pvt.Ltd for 2 years where I've had the privilege of leading multiple Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) projects. I completed my undergraduate studies at Bangalore University in 2021 with a major in Computer Applications.

Currently, I'm pursuing a Master's degree in Cybersecurity at the University of Maryland (UMD), where I am deepening my expertise in offensive security and red-teaming. My current focus is on malware analysis, which I am exploring through an ongoing homelab project.

My ultimate goal is to keep the internet safe by uncovering vulnerabilities before malicious actors can exploit them. I'm committed to making the digital world a safer place, one vulnerability at a time.
